Best Garlicky Caesar Salad


The perfect summer salad, served either on it's own with some grilled chicken or steak.

Goes great with our smoked ribs recipe!

Ingredients

  • Three heads of romaine lettuce
  • Olive oil
  • Anchovy paste
  • 3 tablespoons crushed garlic
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Egg yolk
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 2 strips of cooked bacon, crumbled
  • Sourdough croutons

Steps

  1. First, in a medium bowl, add all your ingredients except the lettuce, bacon, and croutons
  2. Whisk vigorously until well combined
  3. Set bowl aside, and wash your lettuce
  4. Chop the lettuce into bite sized pieces, limiting the amount of heart on each piece
  5. Toss lettuce in with dressing
  6. Add croutons and bacon, toss again.
  7. Serve immediately, top with cracked black pepper and a wedge of lemon to taste
