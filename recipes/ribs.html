Smoked Ribs


This recipe is a great summer dish that works well for a date night, or even when you're having guests over!

This recipe can easily be made with beef or pork ribs, depending on dietary concerns.

Ingredients

  • Rack of ribs, beef or pork
  • Olive oil
  • Brown sugar
  • Apple juice
  • Paprika
  • Mustard
  • Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. First, strip the silverskin off the back of the ribs, and pat them dry
  2. Next, cover liberally with mustard
  3. Then, mix your dry seasonings together, and rub onto ribs
  4. Let ribs rest in the fridge
  5. Preheat smoker to 250F
  6. Mix 3 parts apple juice with one part olive oil, and pour into spray bottle
  7. Smoke ribs for 2 hours, spraying with juice and oil mix every 20 minutes
