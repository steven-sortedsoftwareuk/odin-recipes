Smoked Ribs

This recipe is a great summer dish that works well for a date night, or even when you're having guests over!

This recipe can easily be made with beef or pork ribs, depending on dietary concerns.

Ingredients

  • Rack of ribs, beef or pork
    • Olive oil Brown sugar Apple juice Paprika Mustard Cayenne pepper

Steps

    First, strip the silverskin off the back of the ribs, and pat them dry Next, cover liberally with mustard Then, mix your dry seasonings together, and rub onto ribs Let ribs rest in the fridge Preheat smoker to 250F Mix 3 parts apple juice with one part olive oil, and pour into spray bottle Smoke ribs for 2 hours, spraying with juice and oil mix every 20 minutes
